A menu bar providing commands and options for your application.

## Anatomy

Import the component and assemble its parts:

```tsx filename="Anatomy"
<Menubar>
  <Menu>
    <MenuTrigger />
    <MenuPopup>
      <MenuItem />
      <MenuSeparator />
      <MenuGroup>
        <MenuGroupLabel />
      </MenuGroup>
      <MenuRadioGroup>
        <MenuRadioItem />
      </MenuRadioGroup>
      <MenuCheckboxItem />
      <MenuSubmenu>
        <MenuSubmenuTrigger />
      </MenuSubmenu>
    </MenuPopup>
  </Menu>
</Menubar>
```
